Our Story

Founded by two lifelong gamers frustrated by the lack of integration between the games we love and the potential of blockchain technology. Sidebet is dedicated to solving problems that plague the online gaming industry.

We've harnessed the power of Bitcoin, Discreet Log Contracts and emerging L2 technologies to facilitate instantaneous, peer-to-peer wagering that enables gaming companies to tap into the millions of modern, crypto-savvy players.
